About Shaun the Sheep

The cleverest and funniest sheep at Mossy Bottom

Shaun's characteristics

  1. Shaun is a confident sheep.
  2. Like all the sheep he communicates entirely through bleating.
  3. He often explains his ideas to the flock by drawing diagrams on a blackboard.
  4. He has a good friendship with Bitzer, though this does not stop him from playing pranks on him at times.
